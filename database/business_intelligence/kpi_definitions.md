1. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

To support the "Early Spoilage Detection" objective, the following KPIs have been defined:

KPI Name

Definition

Business Goal

Spoilage Risk Rate

% of active batches with a risk_score > 80.

Keep below 5%. Indicates storage health.

Mean Time to Mitigate (MTTM)

Average time between ALERTS.created_at and MITIGATION_LOGS.action_date.

Ensure staff responsiveness (Target < 2 hours).

Alert Frequency by Crop

Count of alerts grouped by crop_name.

Identify which crops are most difficult to store.

Sensor Compliance

% of batches receiving daily readings.

Ensure hardware reliability.
